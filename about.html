About US/History

Government Jubilee High School is a public high school in Sunamganj District, Bangladesh. This historical School was established in 1887 and was nationalized in 1928. It is situated in the middle of Sunamganj on the bank of the river Surma. It operates from level 3 to level 10 (SSC). Over its history, the school has had notable alumni, including philosopher Dewan Mohammad Ajrof and former foreign minister of Bangladesh Abdus Samad Azad.This school is one of the oldest school in Sylhet division and students from different districts also come to study here.

This institution consists 3 main buildings and a school library which is also a separate building. The primary building which was made by British government is still in original shape however time to time modifications take places. There are 5 fields or better say play ground inside the school compound and the main school play ground (also known as "Balur maat " ) just outside the school boundary wall. Annual sport takes place on that field. This a very reputed and reliable school in Sunamganj. The best institution in Sunamganj District for boys. There's been no doubt about this... Is there?
